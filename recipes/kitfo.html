Kitfo

Description

Kitfo is a traditional food from Ethiopia. It is minced lean meat served raw or semi rawn with a blend of pepper spices and thyme.

Ingredients

  • Lean beef
  • Oil
  • Berbere
  • Thyme
  • Clarified and seasoned butter

Steps

  • Warm a pan or skillet
  • Add clarified butter
  • Toss in meat, which has been thoroughly blended with a spoonful of berbere and a spoonful of thyme
  • Cook to desire (although raw is traditional)
